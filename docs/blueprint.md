# **App Name**: Annisa's Digital Stage

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Visually striking hero section with photo, name, job title, and clear CTAs to view the portfolio and download the CV.
- Portfolio Display: Showcase of design projects with high-quality images and brief descriptions.
- Skills Section: Display skills and expertise of Annisa.
- Contact Form: A straightforward contact form allowing visitors to send messages directly.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft Pink Pastel (#FFD1DC) to evoke a feminine yet professional aesthetic.
- Background color: Clean White (#FFFFFF) to ensure a spacious and modern feel.
- Text color: Charcoal Grey (#333333) for readability and a professional look.
- Body and headline font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for a modern, minimalist style. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Utilize ample white space to enhance the sense of elegance and clarity, adhering to a clean and modern design.
- Employ simple, elegant icons for navigation and contact information to maintain visual consistency.
- Incorporate subtle animations such as fading transitions or gentle parallax scrolling effects to add a touch of sophistication without being distracting.
